Know How To Use Our Product

The following is the correct use of the product:

  • After buying, please download an application called Ngombe on Ios and Playstore.
  • After that, remove the cover of the mini solar panel and place it in a sunny place to charge.
  • Alarm and liter settings in the application in the plan menu.
  • You can see how far you reached your target for the day on the water status.
  • The alarm light will turn on according to the time you set in the app.

Ngombe can help you to ensure your water intake and ease of access for your water intake record by storing it in our database digitally.
