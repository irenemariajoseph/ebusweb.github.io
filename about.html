Our Vision

The purpose of this product is to help users to create a drinking schedule while reminding users to drink according to their schedule. In order to help users increase water consumption and adequate water intake for body health.


The Background Of 'Ngombe' Name



Ngombe, from the Javanese language, means to drink. We chose to use the Java language considering the local branding. A unique name and also symbolized by Javanese wayang. We hope that these local products can be seen and trusted in terms of product quality and quantity and can be accepted by all circles of society.
